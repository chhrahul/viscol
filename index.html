MRI Safety Information

MRI Conditional

Non-clinical testing demonstrated that the Visicoil Plantium Based Product is MR Conditional. A patient with this device can be scanned safely in an MR system immediately after placement under the following conditions:

Static magnetic field of 1.5- Tesla and 3- Tesla, only

Maximum spatial gradient magnetic field of 3000 Gauss/cm or less

Maximum MR system reported, whole body averaged specific absorption rate (SAR) of 2-W/kg for 15 minutes of scanning in the Normal Operating Mode of operation for the MR system

Under the scan conditions defined, the Visicoil Plantium Based Product is expected to produce a maximum temperature rise of 1.6 'C after 15-minutes of continuous scanning.

Artifact Information

In non-clinical testing, the image artifact caused by the Visicoil Plantium Based Product extends approximately 8 mm from this implant when imaged using a gradient echo pulse sequence and a 3- Tesla MR system.

EUS GUIDED FIDUCIAL PLACEMENT
FOR
ESOPHAGEAL CANCER

EXCHANGEABLE FNA SYSTEM (22GA) & GOLD LINEAR FIDUCIAL MARKER

Case Presented by:

Marvin Ryou, M.D.

Instructor in Medicine, Advanced Endoscopy
Brigham & Womens Hospital, Boston, MA

History

A 56 year-old male presented with 2 weeks of progressive dysphagia to solid food. Endoscopy revealed a partially obstructing distal esophageal mass, with biopsies confirming adenocarcinoma. Patient was subsequently referred for staging endoscopic ultrasound (EUS) and placement of fiducials for treatment planning and delivery of radiotherapy

Fig 1: Preloaded FNA platform with VISICOIL
fiducials (inset: magnified view of fiducial)

Procedure

Using a radial echoendoscope, the mass was staged as T3 NO. In anticipation of radiation therapy, 2 linear gold fiducials (0.35mm x 1.0 cm) were preloaded separately into one 22ga FNA exchangeable needle with delivery system and one single 22ga FNA needle. Fiducials were delivered to demarcate the proximal and distal borders of the mass.
Key features of this procedure included:

  • Total delivery time less than 4 minutes

  • Exchangeable needles allow for rapid and accurate deployment without scope/catheter repositioning

  • Preloading multiple sterile needles ensures clinical staff safety by avoiding sequential loading of "dirty" needles

Fig 2: Fiducial marking proximal borde
(inset: endoscopic view of cancer)

Post Procedure

Staging PET scan confirmed optimal fiducial positioning relative to the FDG-avid mass. The fiducials were used by the radiation oncologist to guide radiation treatment planning as well as delivery of radiotherapy.

Fig 3: PET shows optimal fiducial positioning
